
### 🔹 Boshlang‘ich daraja (1–10)

1. Berilgan son musbat yoki manfiy ekanini aniqlang.
2. Son juft yoki toq ekanini aniqlang.
3. Ikki sondan kattasini aniqlang.
4. Ikki sondan kichigini aniqlang.
5. Son 10 dan katta yoki kichik ekanini aniqlang.
6. Son 0 ga teng yoki teng emasligini aniqlang.
7. Berilgan son 100 dan katta bo‘lsa "katta", aks holda "kichik" deb chiqaring.
8. Son manfiy bo‘lsa uning modulini, aks holda o‘zini chiqaring.
9. Ikki son teng yoki teng emasligini aniqlang.
10. Son 5 ga karrali yoki yo‘qligini aniqlang.

---

### 🔹 O‘rta daraja (11–20)

11. Uchta sondan birinchisi kattami yoki ikkinchisi kattami aniqlang.
12. Son 18 dan katta bo‘lsa "adult", aks holda "minor" chiqaring.
13. Son 0 dan katta bo‘lsa "positive", aks holda "negative" chiqaring.
14. Son 2 ga bo‘linadigan bo‘lsa "yes", aks holda "no" chiqaring.
15. Ikki sondan qaysi biri 10 ga yaqinligini aniqlang.
16. Son 50 dan katta bo‘lsa uni 2 ga ko‘paytiring, aks holda 2 ga bo‘ling.
17. Son juft bo‘lsa 2 ga oshiring, aks holda 1 ga kamaytiring.
18. Ikki son yig‘indisi 100 dan katta yoki kichik ekanini aniqlang.
19. Son 1 va 10 oralig‘ida bo‘lsa "yes", aks holda "no" chiqaring.
20. Son 3 ga va 5 ga bo‘linadimi yoki yo‘qligini aniqlang (faqat bittasi bilan tekshiring).
